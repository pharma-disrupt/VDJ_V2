      Known limitations
      
      
        
        
      • D-gene calls are inherently low-confidence from protein sequence alone — always labelled "best guess".
        • 
        
      • PDB gaps in crystal structures can truncate extracted sequences.
        • 
        
      • Serverless payload limit: files larger than 4.5 MB are rejected with HTTP 413.
        • 
        
      • Cold starts: first request after inactivity takes 2–3 s.
        • 
        
      • This is a research tool, not a clinical or diagnostic device.
        • 
      
      
    
      
  
      

  
      
    
      
      Built with FastAPI, Biopython's PairwiseAligner, and a
